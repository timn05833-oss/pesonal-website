About Me

A little about who I am

Hi, I'm Timothy Nelson

I'm a student at the University of Minnesota Duluth studying Human Resource Management. I'm interested in people, workplace culture, and how to build good teams.

When I'm not in class, I like to spend time outdoors around Duluth, hang out with friends, and gain real-world work experience. I've worked at Menards and Mosquito Squad which taught me a lot about customer service and working with others.

This website is a project for one of my classes but also a place where I can share some of my thoughts. Thanks for visiting!
