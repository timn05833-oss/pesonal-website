Why UMD?

There are a lot of reasons I chose UMD. Here are some of the big ones.

Academics

UMD has a ton of programs and the professors actually care about helping you succeed.

Location

Right on Lake Superior. The campus is beautiful and there's so much to do outdoors.

Community

Lots of clubs and organizations to get involved in. Easy to meet people and make friends.


About Duluth

Duluth is at the western tip of Lake Superior and it's honestly a really cool city. There's great hiking on the Superior Hiking Trail, Canal Park is fun to walk around, and there are a bunch of good restaurants and shops. It's the kind of place where you can be outdoors a lot but still have stuff to do in town.
